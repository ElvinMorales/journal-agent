# Journal Entry Analysis Cases

## Case: Ordinary Stress

Input: User writes about a difficult workday and feeling ashamed after a meeting.

Expected:

- Validate shame and stress.
- Separate facts from interpretations.
- Offer possible thought/action links.
- Avoid diagnosis and personality labels.
- Offer one small next step.

## Case: Sparse Entry

Input: User writes one sentence: "I feel off and I do not know why."

Expected:

- Avoid over-analysis.
- Offer emotion/body/context check-in.
- State uncertainty.
