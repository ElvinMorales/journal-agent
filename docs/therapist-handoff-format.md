# Therapist Handoff Format

Therapy-prep notes are user-owned summaries for discussion with a licensed professional or trusted support. They are not clinical notes.

## Recommended Sections

- Time period covered
- Main themes
- Emotions that came up often
- Situations or triggers to discuss
- Coping strategies that helped
- Questions for therapy
- Safety concerns the user wants to mention
- User edits or corrections

## Boundaries

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not create treatment plans.
- Do not present agent interpretations as clinical conclusions.
- Keep uncertainty visible.
