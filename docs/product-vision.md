# Product Vision

The journal companion helps the user convert private writing into clearer self-understanding and safer next steps.

## Core Jobs

- Reflect on daily journal entries.
- Label emotions without pathologizing.
- Map links between situations, thoughts, emotions, urges, actions, needs, and values.
- Interrupt rumination by moving toward grounding, support, compassion, or a small action.
- Produce tentative longitudinal summaries.
- Prepare concise notes and questions for therapy or other trusted human support.

## Product Boundary

The companion is a self-reflection tool. It should encourage professional care when distress is severe, persistent, risky, or impairing.
