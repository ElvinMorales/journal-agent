# Copy-Paste Prompts

Use these prompts in ChatGPT mobile with selected excerpts from private notes. Do not paste entries that you do not want processed by the AI service.

## Evening Reflection

```text
Please reflect on the journal excerpt below as a non-clinical journal companion.

Boundaries:
- This is not therapy, diagnosis, crisis counseling, medical advice, treatment planning, or medication guidance.
- Use tentative language.
- Separate facts, interpretations, emotions, needs or values, and possible next steps.
- Do not assign disorder labels or personality labels.
- If the excerpt includes immediate safety concerns or crisis indicators, stop ordinary reflection and prioritize human support and emergency/crisis resources.

Journal excerpt:
[paste selected excerpt here]
```

## Quick Check-In

```text
Please help me do a brief check-in on this journal note.

Return:
- What I might be feeling
- What may be needing attention
- One small next step
- One gentle question

Keep it tentative and non-clinical. Do not diagnose or give medical advice.

Journal note:
[paste selected note here]
```

## Facts vs Interpretations

```text
Please help me separate facts from interpretations in this excerpt.

Use this structure:
- Facts I wrote or directly observed
- Interpretations or assumptions
- Feelings that may be present
- Possible needs or values
- One small, reversible next step

Keep the response tentative. Do not diagnose, label my personality, or create a treatment plan.

Journal excerpt:
[paste selected excerpt here]
```

## Therapy Prep

```text
Please help me prepare user-owned notes for a therapy session from the selected excerpts below.

Boundaries:
- Do not diagnose.
- Do not create a treatment plan.
- Do not give medication or medical advice.
- Frame everything as possible topics, patterns, questions, or concerns I may want to discuss with a licensed professional.
- If there are immediate safety concerns, prioritize human support and emergency/crisis resources.

Return:
- Topics I may want to discuss
- Possible patterns I noticed
- What seemed to help
- Questions I may want to ask
- Safety or support concerns to mention

Selected excerpts:
[paste selected excerpts here]
```

## Reflection Pasteback Cleanup

```text
Please convert the reflection below into concise first-person notes I can paste back into my private journal.

Keep only user-owned language. Remove anything that sounds like diagnosis, certainty about causes, treatment planning, or clinical conclusions.

Reflection:
[paste reflection here]
```
