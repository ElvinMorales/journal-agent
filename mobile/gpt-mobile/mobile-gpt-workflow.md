# Mobile GPT Workflow

Use ChatGPT mobile as the reflection layer for selected journal excerpts. Keep private storage in Obsidian Mobile or another user-controlled notes app.

## Custom GPT Setup

Configure the custom GPT with repo artifacts such as:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `GUARDRAILS.md`
- `PRIVACY.md`
- `prompts/evening-review.md`
- `prompts/therapy-prep.md`
- Selected templates from `templates/`

Do not upload raw journal entries, private memory, state, exports, crisis notes, therapy notes, secrets, or identifying details as knowledge files.

## Daily Use

1. Write in Obsidian Mobile.
2. Select a specific excerpt.
3. Paste it into ChatGPT mobile with a prompt from `copy-paste-prompts.md`.
4. Ask for tentative reflection, not diagnosis or treatment advice.
5. Review the answer.
6. Paste useful parts back into Obsidian with `reflection-pasteback-template.md`.

## Response Expectations

Helpful responses should:

- Validate emotions without overstating certainty.
- Separate facts, interpretations, emotions, needs, values, urges, and options.
- Use tentative language.
- Offer small, reversible next steps.
- Avoid diagnosis, personality labels, treatment plans, medication guidance, and crisis counseling.

## Crisis Boundary

If an entry includes immediate danger, current self-harm intent, inability to stay safe, danger from another person, or another crisis indicator, stop ordinary reflection. Prioritize immediate human support, emergency services when danger may be imminent, and crisis resources such as 988 in the U.S.

## Privacy Boundary

- Copy only what is needed for the reflection.
- Review text before sending it.
- Avoid screenshots.
- Avoid shared devices.
- Be careful with mobile app history, sync, and account access.
