# Therapy Prep Shortcut Spec

Purpose: help a user open the right notes and prompts before a therapy appointment.

This is a specification only. It is not an importable shortcut file.

## Recommended Actions

1. Ask for the time period to review, such as `past week` or `since last session`.
2. Open Obsidian Mobile.
3. Open the therapy-prep note or create a new private note from a template.
4. Copy the therapy-prep prompt from `mobile/gpt-mobile/copy-paste-prompts.md`.
5. Optional: open ChatGPT mobile.
6. Optional: open calendar or reminders for appointment context.

## User-Owned Output

The output should be framed as user-owned notes for discussion, not clinical findings. Keep language tentative:

- "Possible theme"
- "Something I may want to ask"
- "Concern to mention"
- "What seemed to help"

## Suggested Sections

```markdown
# Therapy Prep

Time period:

## Topics I may want to discuss

## Patterns I noticed

## What helped

## Questions I want to ask

## Safety or support concerns to mention
```

## Safety Boundary

This workflow is not therapy, diagnosis, crisis counseling, medical advice, treatment planning, or medication guidance.

If notes include immediate safety concerns, current self-harm intent, inability to stay safe, or danger from another person, prioritize immediate human support, emergency services when danger may be imminent, and crisis resources such as 988 in the U.S.

## Privacy Boundary

Keep therapy-prep notes in a private vault or private storage location. Do not commit filled therapy-prep notes to the public repo.
