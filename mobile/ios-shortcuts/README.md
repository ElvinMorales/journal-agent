# iOS Shortcuts

This folder contains human-readable iOS Shortcut specifications, not actual `.shortcut` files.

Use them to manually build personal shortcuts on your phone. Review each action before use, especially any action that sends text to another app or service.

## Included Specs

- `quick-capture-shortcut-spec.md`: dictate or type a thought and append it to a daily note or capture file.
- `evening-reflection-shortcut-spec.md`: open the right mobile apps and prompts for a no-code evening review.
- `therapy-prep-shortcut-spec.md`: prepare user-owned notes before a therapy session.

## Boundary

Shortcuts can store highly sensitive journal text. Keep generated files in private local or personal cloud storage. Do not commit captured text or filled notes to the public repo.

If a capture includes current crisis indicators, do not rely on a shortcut or AI reflection as the main support path. Contact trusted human support, emergency services when danger may be imminent, or crisis resources such as 988 in the U.S.
